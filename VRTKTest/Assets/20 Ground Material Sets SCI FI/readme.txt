There is always need for more variations in ground textures! This package contains twenty different types of tileable ground material sets with textures including bump, diffuse and fx maps. There are also versions for terrain texturing.
The textures are divided in two categories: space and alien. The textures come in JPG and PNG formats. 
AND over 60 predefined materials for instant use and testing show you how the textures can work together. 
All textures come in 1024 x 1024 pixels.
